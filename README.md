# data_story_3

This project is a deepdive on SDG 1 (No Poverty) and SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth). Before the deepdive, it also includes history of SDG as a whole and a brief overview of SDG 1 and SDG 8. 

My Data Story 6 is a project on SDG 1 and SDG 8 and how they are intertwined. Intutively, Economic growth is connected to employment and higher employment means no poverty will prevail. This is essentially not the case. There are countries where employment rate is high but poverty is also high.
